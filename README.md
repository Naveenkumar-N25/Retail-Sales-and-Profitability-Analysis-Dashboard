📊 Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes Retail Sales data (2020–2021) to evaluate business performance across revenue, profitability, product demand, sales channels, and regional distribution.

The objective of this project is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights using data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard design.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall revenue and profit performance
* Track monthly sales trends and growth patterns
* Identify top-performing products and regions
* Evaluate profitability across sales channels
* Understand the relationship between revenue, margin, and volume

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI**
* **Power Query**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**

---

## 🔹 Data Preparation (Power Query)

* Cleaned and transformed raw sales data
* Standardized date formats and corrected data types
* Removed inconsistencies and ensured data quality
* Prepared dataset for time intelligence calculations

---

## 🔹 Data Modeling

* Built a structured data model
* Created calculated columns for category grouping (if applicable)
* Ensured relationships and proper aggregation logic

---

## 🔹 DAX Measures Created

### Core KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Operating Profit
* Operating Margin %
* Total Units Sold
* Average Selling Price (ASP)

### Growth Metrics

* Year-over-Year (YoY) Revenue Growth %
* Month-over-Month (MoM) Revenue Growth %
* Running Total Revenue

### Performance Metrics

* Product Contribution %
* Channel Contribution %
* Profit per Unit

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visuals

The report contains 5 main visuals:

1. **KPI Cards** – Executive summary metrics
2. **Monthly Revenue Trend (Line Chart)** – Sales growth over time
3. **Revenue by Product (Bar Chart)** – Product performance comparison
4. **Region-wise Performance (Shape Map)** – Geographic sales distribution
5. **Profitability Analysis (Scatter Plot)** – Revenue vs Margin analysis

### 🎛 Interactive Features

* Date slicer (Year/Month)
* Region slicer
* Sales Method slicer
* Dynamic filtering across visuals

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Identified high-revenue product categories driving overall performance
* Observed regional sales concentration and performance differences
* Detected monthly sales patterns and seasonal trends
* Compared sales channel profitability (In-store vs Outlet)
* Analyzed revenue vs margin relationship using scatter visualization
